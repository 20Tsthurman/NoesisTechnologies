About Noesis Technologies

Custom technology solutions built on real-world experience

Hi, I'm Tanner Thurman

I'm the founder of Noesis Technologies and a Software Engineer at Vanderbilt University Medical Center. With a degree in Computer Science from Western Kentucky University, my specialty is building custom tools that help doctors, researchers, and business owners work more efficiently.

I started Noesis because I saw a gap: small businesses need the same powerful, custom technology as large organizations, but most agencies are either too expensive or push generic solutions that don't quite fit.

My approach is straightforward: I listen to what's slowing you down, figure out the best technical solution, and build something that solves the real problem. Based in Franklin, Tennessee, I'm focused on delivering solid technology that works.
